Welcome to the Worcester Game Pile

The Worcester Game Pile (Woo! Game Pile, for short) is a Central MA meetup for indie, studio, student, aspiring, and potential gamedevs. Whether you've been making games since Infocom was making text-adventures in downtown Cambridge, or are taking your first Game Design course in Highschool, this is a great place to meet fellow developers. Don't make games? That's okay too, we're always happy to meet people curious about what the journey to releasing a videogame is all about.
